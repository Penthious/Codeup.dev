My passions are Skateboarding, Gaming and programming.

I love to skateboard I have been skating for over 7 years now, at one point I thought I was going to become pro but I decided to have my first baby, from there I had to give up skating mostly as I had to work full time and be home to help my wife take care of them. So that in-turn got me really involved in the gaming scene, where I put most of my time into minecraft and League of legends. I finaly decided to better my life and that in turn led me to find my last passion: Programming.

The reason why I am programing.

So I never thought I was going to be a programmer but I met this guy named Colby he got me into programming and from there I tried to be self taught but with me having children it was hard to concetrate on the work at hand. I then heard of Codeup and said I am going to go. I got everything ready but I find out my wife is having another baby. So I needed money again to support a new member in my family. A year later and I had saved up enough money to go to Codeup, I decided to give up games entierly and focuse on programming. I then applied to Codeup and got accepted. That is where I am now
